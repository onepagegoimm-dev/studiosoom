The method

About Gyrotonic®

A flowing, breath-led system of exercise that frees the joints and reconnects the whole body.

The Gyrotonic® Method is a distinctive approach to movement built around circular, spiralling and undulating patterns. Rather than working muscles in isolation, it moves the body through continuous sequences that mirror the way we move in daily life — and in nature.

Movement without strain

Exercises are performed on specialised equipment that supports the body while allowing the joints to move freely through their full range. Because there are no jarring stops or heavy impact, the work is suitable for every age and ability — from those recovering from injury to athletes seeking finer control.

Guided by the breath

Each movement is paired with a specific breathing pattern. The breath sets the pace, stimulates the nervous system and helps release deep-held tension, so the body learns to move with ease rather than effort.

What you can expect

With regular practice, students often notice greater joint mobility, improved posture and circulation, more functional strength and a lasting sense of lightness and balance. Sessions at Studio Soom are private and tailored, so the work always meets you where you are.
