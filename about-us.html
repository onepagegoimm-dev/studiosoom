About us

Led by breath, guided by experience

Studio Director · Go-woon Song

A decade of mindful movement

Studio Soom is led by Go-woon Song, a certified Gyrotonic® and Gyrokinesis® trainer and senior movement instructor with more than ten years of teaching experience.

Her background spans Pilates, strength training and competitive bodybuilding, giving her a rare understanding of how the body builds power while staying supple and aligned. That blend shapes every session at Studio Soom — precise, attentive, and always rooted in the breath.

The studio takes its name from the Korean word for breath. It reflects a simple belief: when movement is guided by mindful breathing, it becomes transformative.
