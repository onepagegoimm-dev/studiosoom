About Gyrotonic®

Movement that flows with the breath

The Gyrotonic Method is a unique system of exercise built around circular, flowing movement. Joints are guided through their natural range without strain, while the breath sets the rhythm — stimulating the nervous system, easing tension and building strength from the inside out.

Every session is continuous and fluid, leaving the body feeling longer, lighter and more connected.

About our studio

Where breath becomes movement

“Soom” means breath in Korean. At Studio Soom we believe breath is the key that unlocks the body's full potential — movement becomes transformative when it is guided by mindful breathing and an inner sense of connection.

Sessions are led by our studio director, a certified Gyrotonic® & Gyrokinesis® trainer and senior movement instructor with more than a decade of teaching experience.
